Privacy policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

This notice explains what personal data I collect through this website, why I use it, and the rights you have over it. It's written to be clear rather than legalistic.

1. Who is responsible for your data

The data controller is Ksenia, an independent marketing and development professional. If you have any question about your data or want to exercise your rights, contact me at xenonline77@gmail.com or on Telegram at @xenaja.

2. What data I collect

Only the details you choose to enter in the contact form:

  • your name;
  • a contact for a reply (Telegram, phone or email);
  • the message you write about your project – optional.

I don't collect special categories of data, and the site runs no hidden trackers (see section 7).

3. Why I use it, and the legal basis

I use your data for one purpose only: to reply to your enquiry and discuss a possible project. Under the GDPR, the legal bases are your consent (which you give by ticking the box when you submit the form) and, where relevant, taking steps at your request before entering into a contract. I never use your details for marketing and never sell them to anyone.

4. Who else processes it

Your message reaches me through a small number of service providers acting as processors on my behalf:

  • Cloudflare – technically forwards the submitted form;
  • Telegram – the messenger where I receive and keep the message.

They only handle the data needed to deliver your message to me.

5. International transfers

Because these providers operate globally, your data may be processed on servers located outside your country, including outside the EEA and the UK. Where that happens, the transfer relies on the safeguards offered by those providers and on your consent to use this contact channel. If you'd rather not, you can simply email me directly instead of using the form.

6. How long I keep it

I keep your message for as long as I need it to handle your enquiry and any resulting work, and I delete it once that's done or whenever you ask me to.

7. Cookies and analytics

This site sets no cookies and runs no analytics or advertising trackers. Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts, which may receive your IP address as a normal part of serving the font files.

8. Your rights

You can ask me to access, correct, delete, restrict or transfer your data, or object to its use, and you can withdraw your consent at any time. Just email xenonline77@gmail.com and I'll respond within a reasonable time. If you're in the EEA or the UK and believe I've mishandled your data, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

9. Changes to this notice

I may update this notice from time to time. The current version is always the one on this page, with the date shown at the top.
