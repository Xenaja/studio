Privacy policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

1. General provisions

This Policy sets out how personal data is processed and the measures taken to protect it. The personal-data operator is Ksenia Vasilyevna Telegina, a self-employed professional (the "Operator"). By using the contact form on the site, a visitor confirms their consent to this Policy and to Russian Federal Law No. 152-FZ "On Personal Data".

2. What data is processed

The Operator processes only the data you voluntarily provide in the request form:

  • name;
  • contact for a reply (Telegram, phone or email);
  • the text of your message (a description of the task) – if you filled it in.

The site does not collect special categories of personal data.

3. Purposes of processing

The data is used solely to contact you about your request: replying to your message, discussing the task, and agreeing on the terms of work.

4. Legal basis

Processing is carried out on the basis of your consent, which you give by ticking the corresponding checkbox when submitting the form.

5. Storage and transfer of data

A request from the site is delivered to the Operator via the Telegram messenger. Technical delivery of the message is provided by Cloudflare (as a processor of the transfer) and by Telegram's servers. The Operator does not share your data with third parties for marketing purposes and does not publish it.

6. Retention periods

The data is kept in the Operator's correspondence for as long as necessary to process your request and for any potential cooperation, and is deleted once the purposes of processing are achieved or at your request.

7. Your rights

You have the right to request access to your data, its correction or deletion, and to withdraw your consent to processing. To do so, write to the Operator: kwag@yandex.ru. Your request is handled within the time frames established by law.

8. Cookies and analytics

At present the site does not use analytics systems or retargeting pixels.

9. Changes to the policy

The Operator may update this Policy. The current version is always available on this page.
